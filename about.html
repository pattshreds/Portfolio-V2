Hello,

My name is Patrick, and I've harbored a passion for technology since a young age. Alongside this, I possess a strong interest for organization, functionality, and design. Having gone through many relocations during my upbringing, I thrive on adapting to new environments. My enthusiasm for perpetual learning, coupled with my desire to share knowledge, fuels my personal and professional growth as an engineer and as an individual.

My professional journey began with my pursuit of a college degree. Like many, I initially lacked confidence in my choice of major. Faced with numerous interests and pressures, I ultimately opted for a degree in Graphic Design. Throughout my college years, I honed my skills not only as an artist but also as a logical and efficient thinker and communicator, delving into the intricacies and psychological aspects of design.

After realizing my true passion lay more in technology than art, I transitioned from graphic design to software development. Drawn to the blend of logical problem-solving and creative architechture involved in software engineering, I enrolled in a full-time Software Engineering bootcamp at General Assembly. Through this immersive experience, I found satisfaction in the logical and critical thinking that software development demands, while still being able to exercise my creativity through crafting responsive layouts, interactive features, and animations. I'm currently pursuing my professional growth as a Software Engineer. Leveraging my background in Graphic Design, I aim to infuse creativity into my work, differentiating myself from the average engineer in this competitive field.
