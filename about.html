Hello,

my name is Patrick. Technology is something that I've always felt passionate about from as of an age young as I can remember. Fortunately enough, so were organization, functionality, and design. Having moved quite a few times growing up, I find adapting to new environments exciting. My Eagerness to be continually learning, and my stoke to share my knowledge both sustain my drive to grow not only as an engineer, but as a human being.

My journey into the professional world began with the pursuit of a college degree. Entering college, like so many others, I was not confident in the major I chose. There were many topics I was interested in and felt the pressure so I chose to obtain a degree in Graphic Design. I spent my time in higher education building my skills as not only an artist, but also a logical and efficient thinker learning about the complexities and the psychology of design.

Eventually I ended up realizing that I found more passionate in the technology I was using over the art itself. I had taken and enjoyed a couple web design courses in college, so I decided my next step would be continuing down that road. I embarked on this new path in my professional journey by making the decision to get a high quality education at General Assembly, where I attended a full-time Software Engineering bootcamp. Having thoroughly enjoyed the logical and critical thinking aspect of building software while still exercising my creative muscles by creating responsive and attractive layouts, features, animations, etc., I knew that this would be a fantastic path for me to follow. Today, I am pursuing my professional evolution as a Software Engineer, using my talents in Graphic Design to help me and my work stand out among the crowd.
